### Email & Communication Automation

**Smart Email Scheduling**  
Our AI analyzes recipient availability, time zones, and response patterns to schedule emails at optimal times. Increase open rates by 40% and reduce back-and-forth by 70%.

**Automated Email Responses**  
Handle routine inquiries instantly with context-aware AI responses. Escalate complex issues to humans automatically.

**Intelligent Email Routing**  
Automatically categorize, prioritize, and route incoming emails to the right team members based on content and urgency.

**Meeting Coordination**  
Schedule meetings across multiple participants and time zones in seconds, not days. Our AI finds optimal times and sends calendar invites automatically.

### HR Operations Automation

**Employee Onboarding**  
Automate the entire onboarding process: account creation, documentation, training schedules, and welcome communications. New hires are productive from day one.

**Leave Management System**  
Employees request time off, AI checks team capacity and approves/denies based on your policies. Managers get notified only for exceptions.

**Performance Review Automation**  
Schedule reviews, send reminders, collect feedback, and generate performance reports automatically.

**Payroll Processing**  
Sync attendance, calculate salaries, deductions, and bonuses, and generate payslips—all without manual intervention.

Automatically track certifications, training requirements, and regulatory compliance. Get alerts before deadlines.
