About

Transforming Business Operations Through AI

OpsAutoAI was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: eliminate tedious manual work so businesses can focus on innovation and growth. Today, we’re the leading provider of AI-powered operational automation solutions.

Our Story

We started when our founders, frustrated by spending 60% of their workweek on repetitive tasks like email scheduling, HR paperwork, and data entry, decided there had to be a better way. They built their first automation tool to schedule team meetings intelligently—and saved 10 hours per week instantly.

That success sparked a vision: What if every repetitive business operation could be automated?

Today, OpsAutoAI serves over 500 companies worldwide, automating everything from employee onboarding to financial reporting. Our AI models have processed over 50 million tasks, saving our clients a combined 2 million work hours annually.

What We Believe

Humans Should Do Human Work - Strategy, creativity, and relationship-building shouldn’t compete with data entry and email sorting.

AI Should Be Accessible - Every business, regardless of size or technical expertise, deserves enterprise-grade automation.

Operations Should Be Seamless - The best automation is invisible—it just works, in the background, every time.

Our Technology

OpsAutoAI’s platform combines:

  • Natural Language Processing - Understand emails, documents, and requests like a human would
  • Machine Learning - Learn your processes and optimize them continuously
  • Intelligent Scheduling - Coordinate across time zones, calendars, and priorities automatically
  • Smart Workflows - Route tasks, trigger approvals, and manage exceptions without human intervention
  • Predictive Analytics - Forecast bottlenecks and recommend process improvements
